## Data We Collect

To provide moderation and anti-abuse features, Cypress collects:

- **Hardware identifiers (HWID)**: used to enforce bans and verify moderator identity. Stored as one-way hashes.
- **EA account identifiers**: your EA persona ID and display name, used to link your Cypress account to your EA account and verify game ownership.
- **Game entitlement IDs**: fetched from EA's API on each login to verify which PvZ games you own. Used solely for access control.
- **IP addresses**: used for rate limiting and abuse prevention. Not stored long-term.
- **Player names**: visible to other players and server hosts during gameplay.

We do not sell, share, or monetize any collected data.
